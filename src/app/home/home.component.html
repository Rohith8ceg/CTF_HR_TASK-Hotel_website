Welcome To Muruga Bhavan

This ornate, palatial tribute to Southern India's greatest empires – Muruga Bhavan – overlooks verdant foliage in the heart of Chennai. It embodies the highest standards in Indian hospitality balanced with elegant restraint in a prime property with distinct personality. Our best fine-dining restaurants, will make you travel the world. From delicious Italian to flavourful South Indian, to the layered spices of authentic Chinese, you savour each experience crafted to perfection.

Muruga Bhavan outshines the needs of the business traveller as well as the bespoke requirements of the destination connoisseur. It is an ode to the majestic palaces of the Chettinad dynasty, courtesy of its grandeur and space. Also, its exclusive location near the beach makes it the perfect venue for all your dream events and celebrations.

Muruga Bhavan, one of the best 5 star hotel in Chennai and one of its best known landmarks, presents a rich fusion of South Indian design and classic elegance. With a location as central as Nungambakkam, you have easy access to the business district, the US Consulate, and key shopping hubs like Mahatma Gandhi Road, Express Avenue, Chennai City Centre, Khader Nawaz Khan Road and T Nagar.

It stands out for yet another reason – the array of delectable dishes it serves in an ambience that reflects the ancient lore of Indian arts and culture.. While we play perfect host to those on business trips to Chennai, the hotel’s romantic Jacuzzi suites and wide variety of timeless fine-dining restaurants make it the ideal spot for those looking for leisure. Wine and dine at this culinary powerhouse, the preferred choice for visiting and local connoisseurs. Choose from authentic Italian at Prego, traditional South Indian delicacies followed by filter coffee at Southern Spice, and the best Chinese delights on the menu at Golden Dragon.

Feed your midnight cravings at the buffet at Anise, our all-day diner, or shake a leg to some music while you sip on our signature cocktails at Chipstead. Pamper yourself and escape the city with spa treatments at our Jiva Grande Spa, your getaway to holistic rejuvenation. Watch the sun set over the city from the exclusive Muruga Club Lounge and pick up an exquisite reminder of India from the treasure trove at Muruga Khazana. Our concierge can help arrange a bespoke sightseeing and shopping experience.
